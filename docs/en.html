Core Concepts

  • GM: Manages world and story.
  • Players: Control characters.
  • Stats: STR, AGI, INT, WIS.
  • HP: 10 + STR.
  • MP: 10 + WIS.

Character Creation

  • Roll 4d6 for stats, drop lowest.
  • Pick basic weapon and armor.
  • Choose a special ability.

Gameplay

  • Turns based on AGI.
  • Actions: Move, Attack, Item, Spell.

Checks & Outcomes

  • Skill Checks: 1d6 + stat.
  • Combat: 1d6 + STR/AGI.
  • Magic: 1d6 + WIS.

DM Tips & Tricks

  • Be Adaptable: Be ready to adjust your plans.
  • Engage Players: Use NPCs to offer insights and resources.
  • Fairness: Balance challenges for a more enjoyable game.
  • Improv: Use "Yes, and..." to make the game more engaging.
